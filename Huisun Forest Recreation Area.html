A Symphony of Green & Soil. Escape the city stress, reconnect with nature, learn sustainable farming, and support local communities in a 72-hour deep immersion.

Start Your Healing Journey

The Three Pillars of the Tour

Green Healing

Step into the forest. Let the phytoncides reduce your stress and heal your urban fatigue through mindful nature walks.

Agri-Education

From bean to cup. Experience the century-old Arabica coffee culture of Huisun and understand sustainable farming practices.

Local Inclusion

Travel with purpose. Support the local indigenous communities and farmers to ensure our journey preserves cultural heritage.

Travel Responsibly With Us

  • 🚌
    Transport: Eco-friendly routing using public transit (Nantou Bus) and low-carbon walking to minimize emissions.
  • 🏡
    Lodging: Stay at Huisun’s certified eco-cabins to maximize your connection with nature while minimizing environmental footprint.
  • ♻️
    Action: Strict Leave No Trace (LNT) policy and zero-plastic dining to preserve the beautiful ecosystem of Taichung and Nantou.

The 72-Hour Experience

Day 1The Connection (Introduction)

Transition from chaos to peace. Arrive at our eco-friendly cabin and begin with a "Century of Aroma" coffee tasting, easing into the tranquil pace of the forest.

Day 2The Heart (Deep Immersion)

Dive deep into biodiversity. Experience guided forest bathing, visit a local coffee farm in Guoxing Township, and end the night observing the magical glow of fireflies (seasonal).

Day 3The Synthesis (Local Impact)

Leave a positive trace. Visit a nearby tribal community and farmers' market. Share traditional food and purchase sustainable souvenirs directly from local hands.
